Shopping Game Rules!
You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four bags below. By clicking on the bags, you will add a specific amount to your shopping cart.
You win the game by matching your total costs to the given number in the beginning. You lose the game if your total costs goes above the given number.
The costs of each bags is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the costs of the each bags.
